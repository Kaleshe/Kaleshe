Mublo

Problem

Sometimes it can be difficult for independent artists and/or labels to track when and where their songs have been played. This is currently done through multiple avenues. Such as music royalty companies for live perfomances.

The problem with this is that itâ€™s hard to keep up with, This means that artists and/or labels are always losing out on potential revenue.

Solution

Mublo, an application that allows you to register your music into its database. Crediting all who were involved in its production. using Blockchain

A smart contract is created once a registered song has been played and detected by an aggregator. The royalty for the play is then split between the aggregator and everyone involved in its production.
