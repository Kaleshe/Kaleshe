The Rushey Green Group Practice

We are a 4 partner General Medical Practice in the heart of Catford, London. We currently have 3 salaried GPs, 2 Permanent Locum GP's, 2 Nurse Practitioner’s, 1 Physician Associate, 3 Practice Nurses, 2 Health Care Assistant and a dedicated administrative team.

We offer a complete range of medical services including maternity care, baby clinics, immunisations, general medical and surgical care, minor surgery, contraception, screening and health promotion for both men and women.
